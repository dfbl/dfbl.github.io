I'm a senior at Old Dominion University majoring in computer science with an interest in web development. Growing up, I was introduced to front-end design thanks to a little "virtual pet-site" called Neopets. It's important for me to always try to center and uplift marginalized folks whenever possible, both in my activism and in my work.
